I was honored to receive the Edison Medal from the American Institute of Electrical Engineers. This award celebrates my achievements in electrical engineering, and I consider it one of my most prestigious honors.

1907(FB) - 125 years ago

My Inventions

1879 - 146 years ago

Electric Light Bulb

My electric light bulb came to life. Using a carbon filament, I produce light when electricity passes through it. Previous bulbs were short-lived, but my bulb can last over 1,200 hours. This made electricity common in homes and workplaces. Now, you don’t have to stay in the dark!

1877 - 148 years ago

Phonograph

I invented the phonograph, the first device that can record sound. I capture sound waves onto a cylinder, allowing people to listen to music and voices later. This started the music industry and changed how people enjoy entertainment!.

1891 - 134 years ago

Motion Picture Device

I developed the kinetoscope. This machine allows one person to watch moving pictures. The kinetoscope lays the groundwork for the film industry and offers people a new entertainment experience. It marks the beginning of cinema history!.
